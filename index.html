Description

eesubmit makes it easier to submit your EE homework.

Installation

  1. git clone https://github.com/DakotaAndMichael/eesubmit.git
  2. cd eesubmit/main
  3. ./macInstall.bash or ./linuxInstall.bash depending on your OS

Usage

  1. Name your scanned hw "hwHand.pdf", where x is your homework number
  2. Change into your hw directory
  3. eesubmit
